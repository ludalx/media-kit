Collaboration formats
01
Mid-roll Integration
A natural pause in the middle of the video where I introduce your brand in my own voice. I focus on weaving the sponsorship into the narrative flow so it feels like a genuine recommendation rather than a disruptive commercial break, keeping audience engagement high.
02
Post-roll Ad Read
A dedicated space at the very end of the video for your message, paired with a clean, branded title card. This gives your brand a clear, uncluttered spotlight while providing a clean separation from the main story.
03
Product Placement
Your product is naturally incorporated into the visual background or routine of the video, with no formal ad read or verbal mention. A highly effective, non-intrusive format that works best for everyday lifestyle items that fit organically into the space.
